Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

How do I create an account?

Click the "Login" button at the top of the page and select "Sign up". You can create an account using your email or sign up with Google or Facebook.

Is it free to use MarketPlace?

Yes, creating an account and browsing items is completely free. We only charge a small fee when you successfully sell an item.

What areas do you serve?

We currently operate in over 100 cities worldwide. Enter your location to see items available in your area.

Buying

How do I contact a seller?

Click the "Contact Seller" button on any listing to start a conversation through our secure messaging system.

Is my payment secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and secure payment processors to protect all transactions.

What if I have issues with my purchase?

Contact the seller first through our messaging system. If you can't resolve the issue, our customer support team is available 24/7.

Selling

How do I list an item?

Click the "+ SELL" button, fill in your item details, add photos, and set your price. Your listing will be live immediately.

What can I sell?

You can sell most legal items. Check our Terms & Conditions for a list of prohibited items.

What are the selling fees?

We charge a 5% fee on successful sales. Listing items is completely free.

Account & Security

How do I reset my password?

Click "Login", then "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions sent to your email.

How do I report a suspicious user?

Click the "Report" button on any user profile or listing, or contact our support team directly.

How do you protect my data?

We use advanced security measures to protect your data. Read our Privacy Policy for detailed information.

Still Have Questions?

Can't find what you're looking for? Our support team is here to help 24/7. Contact Support
